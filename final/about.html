About Our Parks

Montana is known as the “Big Sky Country” because of its overwhelming and unobstructed skyline. It's home to two of the most picturesque national parks in America: Yellowstone National Park and Glacier National Park. However, most people don't know that there are actually a total of nine National Parks in Montana.

Glacier National Park

Yellow Stone National Park

Nez Perce National Historical Park

Big Hole National Battle Field

Grant-Kohrs Ranch National Historic Site

Lewis and Clark National Historic Trail

Fort Union Trading Post National Historic Site

Little Bighorn Battlefield National Monument

Bighorn Canyon National Recreation Area

The purpose of this website is to raise awareness of National Parks across Montana and to provide a site where everyone can contribute by donating money or practicing one of the other methods for preserving our parks.
